Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Overview

Agenr ("we", "us", "our") operates the agenr.ai website and the Agenr API platform. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

When you use Agenr, we may collect:

  • Account information: Email address, name, and profile picture provided through OAuth sign-in (Google or GitHub).
  • API usage data: Request logs, adapter execution metadata, and error information for debugging and service improvement.
  • Credentials you store: API keys and OAuth tokens you choose to connect to adapters. These are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM with AWS KMS envelope encryption.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Agenr platform
  • To authenticate your identity via OAuth providers
  • To securely store and retrieve credentials for adapter execution on your behalf
  • To monitor service health and debug issues
  • To communicate important updates about the service

Data Security

We take security seriously. All stored credentials are encrypted using AES-256-GCM envelope encryption backed by AWS KMS. Session tokens are stored as secure, HTTP-only cookies. We do not store OAuth provider passwords — authentication is handled entirely through OAuth 2.0 flows with Google and GitHub.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth & GitHub OAuth: For user authentication
  • AWS KMS: For credential encryption key management
  • Fly.io: For application hosting
  • Cloudflare: For DNS and static site hosting
  • Turso: For database services

Each provider has their own privacy policies. We only share the minimum information required for these services to function.

Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. API usage logs are retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Disconnect any stored credentials at any time through the Developer Console

Cookies

We use a single session cookie (agenr_session) to maintain your authenticated session in the Developer Console. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at privacy@agenr.ai.
